Policies

Full Service or Staffed Events

• Complete setup of food, including chafers and canned heat.
• Appropriate serving utensils.
• Heavy duty disposable Reflection silver plastic ware wrapped in dinner napkin with ribbon , Heavy duty plastic plates.
• Cloth linens for food station table(s) and or beverage station.
• Appropriate table decorating for the food buffet station or table (based on season and/or client theme with fresh floral arrangements.
• Plastic drinkware for soft drinks and water if needed.
• Complete breakdown and clean up of food areas/kitchen at the end of the function following event venues policies and procedures.
• 18% Gratuity will be added to all events unless requested amount is different.

Drop Off Events

• Delivery and set up of food, including wire Chafers and Canned Heat( if ordered).
• All appropriate serving utensils.
• Reflections or Clear Plastic-ware wrapped in white napkin with black Band and tied with ribbon or Plastic-ware Packet depending on event.
• Paper, styrofoam, or plastic plates ( depending on orders).
• Paper or plastic coffee cups for soft drinks, coffee, and water if needed.
• $25.00 delivery fee
